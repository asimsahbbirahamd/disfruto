Project Overview

This is NOT a complex ecommerce platform.

We have:
- 1 subscription product

- Fixed delivery days (Thu–Sun)
- Berlin-only delivery
- No product variants
- No upsells
- No referral system
- We need a clean, lean MVP built on Shopify within 4 weeks.


Scope of Work

1. Shopify Setup
- Theme setup (clean, editorial look – design direction provided)
- Basic pages (Home, FAQ, Legal, Footer)
- Payment integration (Shopify Payments / Stripe)

2. Subscription Logic
- Using an existing subscription app (e.g. Seal Subscriptions or similar):
- 2-week & 4-week recurring options
- Pause & cancel functionality
- Changes allowed until Sunday before delivery week

3. Delivery Day Selection

- Thursday
- Friday
- Saturday
- Sunday

- Thu–Sat: 17:00–20:00

No need for time slot selection.

4. Account Area / Customer Portal
Customers must be able to:
- View next delivery date
- Change delivery day
- Pause subscription
- Cancel subscription
- Update payment details

Keep it simple. No custom dashboards.
